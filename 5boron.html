BORON

Boron is a non metallic element and the only non-metal of the group 13 of the periodic table the elements. Boron is electron-deficient, possessing a vacant p-orbital. It has several forms, the most common of which is amorphous boron, a dark powder, unreactive to oxygen, water, acids and alkalis. It reacts with metals to form borides.At standard temperatures boron is a poor electrical conductor but is a good conductor at high temperatures. More...

  • Heat of fusion 50.2 kJ/mol

GENERAL USES OF BORON

  • Boron is used as rocket fuels
  • Boron is used as food preserative
  • It is used to make brosilicate glass
  • Its isotope is used in nuclear reactors
